About Me

I hold a BSc (Hons) in Computing and have always been drawn to the intersection of creativity and technology. For me, there's no better fusion of the two than game development.

My journey into 3D started with a personal project — recreating the buildings and spaces of my own college campus in Blender, from scratch. Every texture, every wall, every detail was crafted through careful observation and reference.

Those assets became the foundation of a survival game built in Unity, transforming familiar spaces into an interactive experience. I believe the best games are built on genuine places, stories, and emotion.

Game Projects

🎮 Unity · First Person · Survival Horror

Terror in
the Halls

Your campus. Your nightmare.

A first-person survival horror game set entirely within a 3D recreation of my real college campus — every building, every corridor, every space modelled from scratch in Blender. You are a student. Everyone else is a zombie. Find the antidote before they find you.

▶ Watch Gameplay Play on Itch.io →
🔪

Melee Combat

Arm yourself with a knife or baseball bat. No guns — every encounter requires nerve and timing. Get too close and it's over.

🧪

Search for the Antidote

The antidote is hidden somewhere on campus. Explore familiar spaces — classrooms, lounges, corridors — while avoiding the horde.

👁️

Stealth & Sound

Zombies react to sound. Move carefully, avoid detection, and plan your route. Running attracts attention — and attention gets you killed.

❤️

Health & Inventory

Manage your health and carry limited items. Every decision matters — do you grab the health pack or push deeper into the building?

🏰 Unity · First Person · Puzzle

Kingdom
of Traps

Every room hides a secret.

A first-person puzzle game set inside a mysterious castle. You wake up trapped — and must escape room by room, solving puzzles ranging from finding hidden keys and pressing secret buttons, to navigating laser puzzles and rooms that imprison you for no visible reason. Nothing is what it seems.

▶ Watch Gameplay Play on Itch.io →
🔑

Room Escape Puzzles

Each room presents a unique challenge — find hidden keys, discover secret switches, or uncover interactions that aren't immediately obvious.

🔦

Laser & Mirror Mechanics

Redirect beams of light using mirrors and prisms to unlock sealed doors. Precision and spatial thinking are key.

🧠

Psychological Traps

Some rooms have no obvious exit — the player is trapped with no key, no button, no clue. The solution lies in thinking outside the room itself.

🏰

Castle Exploration

Navigate through interconnected castle chambers — libraries, halls, dungeons, and bedrooms — each with its own atmosphere and puzzle logic.
